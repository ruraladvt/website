Welcome

            Rural Advertising and Marketing is a family based company serving companies from more than 8 years through its Head Office at Muzaffarpur, Bihar and different Branch Offices in different states. Today we distribute and deliver results to all of the major markets across different States in India.


            Rural Advertising and Marketing is one of the fastest-growing areas of advertising and promotions. By reaching out to the targeted audience of our clients choice, advertisers have improved their creative approaches to utilize the strength of direct marketing. This target system is so direct, the advertiser can choose the preferred area where they would like to send their sales promotions.
